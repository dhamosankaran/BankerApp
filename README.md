### 1. Segment-Specific Dashboards

- **Private Banking**: Wealth management, trust planning, and estate metrics.
- **Corporate Banking**: Treasury details, facility tracking, and credit exposure.
- **Institutional**: AUM analysis, ESG focus metrics, and RFP win rates.

- **Action Timeline**: Audit-ready logs of all compliance interventions.
